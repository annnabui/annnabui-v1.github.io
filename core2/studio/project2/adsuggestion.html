EVALUATION:
ChatGPT's algorithm and my Instagram algorithm are very similar in terms of ad suggestions. The majority of ads (36%) were under the Fashion and beauty category, mostly delivered as a post (61.5%). 14% of all ads belongs to the Music and entertainment category. Interestingly, there were no ads for Fitness and wellness, but there were more ads in the Travel and adventure category than I thought (2 stories). I also received ads for Animal and pet (8%) which makes sense because ChatGPT did outline it as one of my main interests. Tattoo-related content was also 8% of total ads.

Overall, I saw that advertisements which appeared as posts are more relevant to my topics, while ads delivered through stories are more experimental. I received ads for wine and champagne which I don’t really care about. I would say this is due to the structural difference between posts and stories – I go through stories faster than I do with posts, so even if I saw irrelevant content on my stories I wouldn’t mind it as much.

I was surprised to see that I received no ads on Knitting or Yarn, because I saved a lot of posts under that topic.
